About

Means of Production was born out of an overwhelming sense of despair (yes, despair) with the state of productivity apps. Some allowed you to create to-do lists; some allowed you to do pomodoros; some allowed you to set reminders; and some allowed you to track your productivity (albeit not meaningfully). But none could do all of these things. Worse yet, none actually made you more productive.

The goal of Means of Production is to remedy all these ailments by being the only truly all-in-one productivity app, and to do it all for free. It's very much a work in progress, and there are many features on the way. If you would like to suggest ideas or improvements, please email me at david@meansapp.com.
